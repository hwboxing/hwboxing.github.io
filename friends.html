Train at Ricky's Gym

Want some more practice or more of a “one-on-one” boxing experience?

Visit Pro Boxing Supplies

We have an account at the Pro Boxing Supplies store in Studio City.


If you shop in the store, let the sales people know of the Harvard-Westlake account and they will sell you items at a discounted price, 10% less than retail.

Support Los Angeles Family Housing

HW Boxing Club supports LA Family Housing, a nonprofit that helps impoverished families and children get back on their feet after hard-times.


Donate to or volunteer at LAFH to support a better life for struggling households.
