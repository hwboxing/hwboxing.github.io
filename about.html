The Harvard-Westlake Boxing Club Mission Statement

We're making it our goal to promote fitness, mental fortitude, and athletic discipline through the training regimen found in the sport of boxing.

We combat lethargy by engaging in the healthy, physical activity involved in endurance training, while learning the various offensive and defensive skills that come with boxing.

We make efforts to better our community through open workshops and community fundraisers to promote awareness and a purpose beyond ourselves.
